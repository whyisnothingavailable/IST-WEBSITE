Hong Eunchae (홍은채; or simply Eunchae) is a South Korean singer under Source Music. She is the maknae of the girl group LE SSERAFIM.

Career

2022: Debut with LE SSERAFIM

On April 6, she was introduced as the third member of LE SSERAFIM.[1] They debuted on May 2, 2022 with the mini album Fearless.
