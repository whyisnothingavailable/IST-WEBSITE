Huh Yunjin (허윤진; or simply Yunjin) is a South Korean-American singer-songwriter and producer under Source Music. She is a member of the girl group LE SSERAFIM.

Career

2018–2019: Produce 48

In 2018, she appeared as a contestant on the survival show Produce 48 where she represented Pledis Entertainment. She was eliminated in episode 11 after ranking 26th place.

Following the conclusion of Produce 48, it was rumored that she and fellow label mate and contestant Kaeun would debut in a new girl group together. But, after Kaeun left Pledis Entertainment, plans for the group's debut fell through, and Yunjin's plans were remained to be discussed.

2021–2022: Source Music, debut with LE SSERAFIM

On August 24, 2021, it was revealed that Yunjin had officially signed a contract with Source Music and would be debuting as a member of the agency's new girl group, LE SSERAFIM, in the near future.

On April 9, 2022, she was introduced as the sixth and final member of LE SSERAFIM. They debuted on May 2, 2022 with the mini album Fearless.

Personal life

Education

On March 4, 2019 Yunjin became a student of the Applied Music Department at Hanlim Multi Art School. However, she later dropped out before she could graduate. Miyawaki joined HKT48 as a first generation trainee in July 2011. She made her first official appearance as an HKT48 member on October 23 at a national handshake event for the song "Flying Get". She made her theater debut on November 26 with a revival of SKE48 Team S stage "Te wo Tsunaginagara".
