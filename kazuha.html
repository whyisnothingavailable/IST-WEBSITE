Kazuha (Korean: 카즈하; Japanese: 一葉/カズハ) is a Japanese singer, rapper and ballerina under Source Music. She is a member of the girl group LE SSERAFIM.

Career

2022: Debut with LE SSERAFIM

On April 8, she was introduced as the fifth member of LE SSERAFIM. They debuted on May 2, 2022 with the mini album Fearless.

Personal life

Education

Kazuha was a professional ballet dancer before her debut and attended Hashimoto Sachiyo Ballet School & Jr in Japan, Bolshoi Ballet Academy in Moscow, Royal Ballet School in the UK, and since 2020 she has been studying at the Dutch National Ballet Academy in the Netherlands, where she was recruited by the CEO of Source Music in mid-December 2021.
